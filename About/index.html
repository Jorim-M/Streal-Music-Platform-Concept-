Here's What We're All About

We’re a digital networking platform, born out of the desire of Kenyan artists to be independent and drive their own journey the way they see best as well as earn through their craft. We want our artists to compete at a better playing field and their music accepted all over.
We are 100% independent and are here to help solo musicians, artists, bands, DJs, performers, producers, and creators who record music at home or in the studio make a living doing what they love.

Too Good To Be True?

Our digital space is an extremely user-friendly platform enabling artists to release unlimited music. Therefore, you own and sell your music, retaining 100% rights and earn 100% of your earnings to your fans.

Build Your Audience

Direct engagement with your listeners all over the world and ensuring they are with you through your entire journey.

Collaborating Just Got A Lot Better

We are telling the African artists stories the African way. With our digital space you artists are able to connect and collaborate with other artists whether in the same genre or different.

For Your Eyes Only

Track your music’s performance and delve into streaming insights, download numbers and listener demographic data from your personal Streal dashboard to help you plan the best next steps for your career.

And Finally, Go Get That Bag! $$$

We build unique online stores and help you run your creative business i.e selling your music merchandise and any souvenir for your fans thus maximizing your income potential. You design, we print and ship it to your fans and send you the profit.
